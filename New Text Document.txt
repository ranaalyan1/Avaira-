HTML Examples

Basic HTML structure:
<html>
<head>
<title> Page Title Goes Here </title>
</head>
<body>
content goes here
</body>
</html>

Adding audio, image and video files:
<html>
<head>
<title>Adding Media</title>
</head>
<body>
<img src="abc.jpg" alt="Sample image" />
<video src="abc.mp4" controls>
  Your browser does not support the video tag.
</video>
</body>
</html>
